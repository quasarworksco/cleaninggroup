About Us

Your Partner in Service

Cleaning Group Inc. is a professional staffing and facility services company headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia. We specialize in delivering top-tier personnel and operational support across the hospitality, food service, and logistics industries.

Our commitment is simple: to be the reliable partner your business depends on. Whether you need seamless hotel operations, flawless event execution, or efficient warehouse management, we bring dedication and expertise to every assignment.
